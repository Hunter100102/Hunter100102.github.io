Pivoting Head and Swinging Armature Project

This project involved integrating two DC motors and a LIDAR radar detector with a Raspberry Pi 4 to create a pivoting head with the LIDAR detector and a swinging armature or "weapon". The purpose of this system was to disqualify contestants if they were still moving when the "head" turned around, facing the playing field, indicating a red light.

I undertook this project alone, from conception to implementation. The main components and steps involved in this project included:

  • Selection and integration of two DC motors and a LIDAR radar detector with a Raspberry Pi 4.
  • Designing and constructing the pivoting head and swinging armature mechanism.
  • Programming the Raspberry Pi 4 to control the motors and interpret data from the LIDAR detector.
  • Testing and refining the system to ensure accurate and reliable operation.
